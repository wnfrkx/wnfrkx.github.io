Numbo Card Generator

made by Julian Gatchalian (Maksci alumnus)







This is a random numbo card generator for the Numbo game played during Math Week. To create a new card configuration, click "Generate" at the bottom of the card.

Taking a Screenshot
To take a screenshot of the card, (1) find Snipping Tool on your laptop, or (2) press the Print Screen button on your keyboard and crop the image.

Placing the Screenshot in Word
The dimensions of the numbo card should be 365 by 438 px, or an aspect ratio of 5:6.
