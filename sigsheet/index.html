My UPMC Sigsheet Level

Are you an applicant wanting to keep track of your sigsheet progress? Or a member trying to remember who you interacted with in UPMC? Julgat has the right tool for you.

This project takes huge inspiration from My Philippines Travel level by Denz here: https://my-philippines-travel-level.com/

How to use

If you are a member, you may refer to our sigsheet tracker to determine who is who in the sigsheet.

If you are an applicant, this is a great way to visualize your progress in the sigsheet!

This self-made map is divided into eight "islands" representing the Executive Committee, the six standing committees and the members on leave as reflected in the 22B sigsheet. Clicking on a "province" in the map will reveal a member's codename and a few phrases.

  • Signature obtained - You got the member's signature during your application process.
  • Task accomplished - You accomplished that member's task and you are awaiting their signature.
  • Basic information known - You collected the basic information needed for that member's place in the sigsheet.
  • Interactions made - You interacted with the member at least once but you never interviewed them for their signature.
  • Social media mutuals - You and the member are social media mutuals. That's it.
  • Never interacted with - You never interacted with this member yet..

Your "sigsheet level" will update in real-time. Plus, your progress in the map will not be erased! This means that, even if you leave this page, you can always come back to this page.
