WELCOME

Welcome to our project website, visitor. This is the frankenstein collaboration project for Grade 11 Earth Sciences made by the Holy Trio (Rocero, Gatchalian, Hermoso).

From reading in-depth information to interactive elements, you are able to choose what topic you are interested in. Feel free to navigate the various content prepared for you by clicking on the links provided.

This project is all about the various lessons that have been taught during our 2nd Quarter classes in Earth Sciences. May you enjoy your stay on our website. Cheers!

Earth's Internal Heat and Magmatism

The content for Week Two contains the Layers of the Earth and its various sub-topics. Notable sub-topics inside this week are: Earth’s Layers, Earth’s Internal Heat, Movement of Heat inside the Earth, Magmatism, and Magma Formation.

Deformation and Metamorphism of Rocks

Alongside with the topic of metamorphism, both contain their respective sub-topics for a more in-depth look. This week stretches along these following concepts: Deformation, Stress, Strain, Types of Stress, Stages of Deformation, Factors that Affect the Behavior of Materials, and Effects of Deformation. It also engulfs these other concepts from metamorphism like: Types of Metamorphism, Types of Metamorphic Rocks, and Metamorphic Grade.

Plate Tectonics

The sub-topics beneath this week are: Exogenic and Endogenic Definitions, Continental Drift Theory, Seafloor Spreading, Tectonic Plates, and the Various Types of Plate Movements.
